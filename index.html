Floodie

Floodie is a powerful discord bot. Features include:

  • !link → creates a temporary, undetectable Cloudflare-powered tunnel to our server. Lasts 10 minutes. Resolves to visitor's real ipv6 address + location.
  • !httpflood → Layer-7 httpflood using our private, concurrent testing servers. Currently concurrent nodes are 2.

Tor-only access · End-to-end encrypted · No third parties
